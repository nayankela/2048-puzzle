2048 Puzzle

How to Play :

  • 2048 is played on a 4Ã—4 grid, with numbered tiles that slide smoothly.
  • Player moves a tile using the four arrow keys.
  • Each time you slide, a new tile will randomly appear in an empty spot on the board.
  • Tiles slide as far as possible in the chosen direction until they are stopped by either another tile.
  • Two tiles of the same number collide while moving, they will merge into a tile.
  • Resulting tile cannot merge with another tile again in the same move.
  • You reach the number 2048 YOU WON!.
  • All the numbers become non-Zero and Not reached to 2048 YOU LOSE.
